Election

One ballot with multiple positions and questions. Voters submit the ballot when the vote is open, and the vote is typically scheduled for a 4 hour to 1 week duration.

Meeting Vote

One vote within a series of votes. Each vote is separately voted on and tallied. Votes are opened by the administrator and end after 2 to 10 minutes.

Announcement

A notice to save the date for a future election or meeting. Optionally register votes or call for candidates or nominations.

Vote Box

Create a Box and add Meeting Votes, Elections or Announcements to group them together. Great for managing meetings or storing votes together.
